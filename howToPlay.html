How To Play

Basics

  1. Click the "Play!" button in the navigation bar on the top of the page.
  2. Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move your sprite around the forest maze.
  3. When you encounter a mushroom, move your sprite over it to pick it up!
  4. When you pick a mushroom, it will disapear and a point will be added to your score.

Goal

Pick up as many mushrooms throughout the maze as you can!
